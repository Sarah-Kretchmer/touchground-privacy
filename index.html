1. No Collection of Personal Data

I do not collect, store, or share any personal data directly from users of TouchGround.

  • The App does not require account registration.
  • The App does not request or store names, email addresses, contact details, health data, or location data.

2. Third-Party Services

The App relies on third-party providers to function:

  • Apple App Store (for downloads, subscription management, and payments).
  • OpenAI (for speech recognition and text/voice generation).
  • Conversations with the chatbot are transmitted directly to OpenAI for processing. I do not access, store, or control this data myself.

Any data transmitted through these services is handled by the provider under their own privacy policies:

  • Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy
  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

I do not control and am not responsible for how these providers process data. Depending on your location, data may be transferred outside the EU.

3. Conversation Transcripts & Insights

When you use the AI Life Coach, your voice is transcribed into text for the purpose of generating a reply. These transcripts are also briefly analyzed to highlight key points you mentioned, which are displayed back to you in the Insights section of the App.

  • I do not store or access these transcripts myself.
  • Transcription and analysis are handled by OpenAI as described above.
  • Insights are generated only for your use within the App and are not shared with me or with other parties.
